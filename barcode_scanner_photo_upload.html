📋 Best Photo Tips:

  • Use iPhone 3x zoom (telephoto lens)
  • Distance: 2-3 feet from barcode
  • Stand to the SIDE (20-30° angle), NOT overhead!
  • Tap barcode to focus before shooting
  • Good lighting - avoid shadows
  • Hold steady - use volume button to shoot
